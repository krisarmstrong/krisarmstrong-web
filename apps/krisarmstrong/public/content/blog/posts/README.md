## Content Characteristics

### Writing Style
- Professional but accessible technical writing
- Written from perspective of experienced network engineer
- Real-world examples and deployment experiences
- Specific numbers, specifications, and measurements
- Avoids marketing fluff, focuses on technical substance
- 800-2,000 words per post (average ~1,600)
